Other Matilda projects

The MMI-1 Promotion is committed.

This individual project dedicated to Katherine Johnson is part of a collective effort by the MMI class to challenge the invisibility of women in scientific fields.

Each student chose a different “Matilda” - researcher, inventor, pioneer - to explore a unique angle of this historical and social phenomenon.
